        SYSTEM GUIDE

        1. Setting Up Your Assets

        Begin by entering your Liquid Assets (Cash or money in the bank).
        • Gold: Enter your physical gold in the Physical Gold Holdings (Liquid) section. These figures automatically populate your Portfolio Snapshot.
        • Fixed Assets: Do the same for long-term investments like property values.

        2. Managing Crypto

        Track your crypto holdings in two steps:
        • Click "Add/Remove Coins" in the Crypto Assets block to choose the coins you hold.
        • Enter the Units you own into the platform blocks. This data will reflect in both the Cryptocurrency Prices table and your Portfolio Snapshot.

        3. Snapshots & History

        Click "Save" at any moment to create a snapshot.
        • The system calculates your percentage change based on your last saved entry.
        • The History list shows percentage changes of all past entries to track your growth.

        4. Transaction Records & PnL

        Use the Transactions container to keep records of your crypto buys.
        • It auto-converts between USD, ZAR, and VND.
        • It provides a full PnL calculation (Profit and Loss) automatically.

        5. Built-in Tools

        • Converters: Use the live Exchange Rates and Gold price conversion tools for quick calculations.
        • News: Stay updated with the Crypto News section, which refreshes regularly with the latest market info.
